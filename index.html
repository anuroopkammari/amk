    Services

    Pulmonology

    Pulmonology is the discipline of medical sciences dealing with the ailments of the respiratory system. AMK Hospitals offer an unparalleled range of holistic health care services for patients with disorders and issues of the respiratory system and the associated organs such as the heart and the vascular system of the body along with the complex molecular processes associated with respiration.

    Cardiac Sciences

    AMK Hospitals provide an all-around tertiary level of cardiac care for patients with a wide spectrum of medical conditions. With the help of state-of-the-art infrastructure equipped with the latest technology, our adept cardiac specialists with globally acclaimed clinical skills and international certifications offer minimally invasive diagnostic and successful surgical procedures

    Neurosciences

    Neurology is the branch of medical sciences that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of a plethora of disorders of the nervous system. AMK Hospitals in India are one of the best healthcare providers for neurological problems in children and adults offering comprehensive diagnosis and management of symptoms of ailments associated with the nervous system including other interdisciplinary research and diagnosis for neuro-vascular, neuro cardiac, and neuro electrophysiology problems among others.

    Medical camps

    A temporary establishment that provides surgical facilities by a group of medical experts headed by a medical officer, which are routinely not available in that region or center.

    Camp1

    Cleaning all surfaces that come in contact with the patient with freshly prepared 0.5% chlorine solution (prepared by mixing 15gm of bleaching powder in a liter of water.)

    Camp2

    Monitoring of quality of health care being provided in camps should be done at all levels starting from the district level to the national level. Indian medical association should be well knit in this structure at every level.

    Camp3

    Reviewing the processes with the help of the checklists on client case audit/ facility audit/observation of sepsis and surgical procedure

    Camp4

    a smaller committee of 4 to 5 members comprising the Medical Superintendent, I/C Surgeon/Physician, IMA appointed nodal officer, I/C OT, Nursing I/C and other key members of staff should be constituted.
